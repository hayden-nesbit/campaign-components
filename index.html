On the Issues

Jack spends a lot of his time on the issues. Unfortunately, those issues are his raging anger, destructive alcoholism, and--with his current stay at the Overlook Hotel--hallucinations and a inevitable mental breakdown. Due to these issues, Jack is unable to comment on issues more relevant to the election.

Fundraising

Jack's campaign finances are a mystery. With no formal campaign team, and with his own worsening mental illness, no one's quiet sure how anything is getting done. Nevertheless, Jack has convinced several people to donate toward his term as Constable which will surely be a terrible and potentially dangerous one.
